Stream at a location and at a time

2 Dec. 2010, rev. 5 Febr. 2012

Introduction

This topic shows how to model a temporal part of a stream at the inlet nozzle of a pump

Model

Temporal part #43734 of myStream #23492 is contained in the inlet nozzle #93843 of myPump #93850 between May 18, 2005 at 2:25:45 pm Z(ulu) time and May 18th, 2005 at 2:26:45Z. That temporal part of myStream is classified with an instance of Property that is quantified as 15.2 on the Scale 'barg'.

A temporal part of myStream has a pressure of 15.2 barg during a period in time at a certain location

NOTE- The detailing of the points in time and the property has not been shown for clarity's sake.
